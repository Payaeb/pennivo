Pennivo is a free, open-source markdown editor built for writers who think in markdown.

Write in a clean, distraction-free WYSIWYG editor that renders your markdown in real time — no preview pane, no split view, just your words on the page. Switch to source mode when you want full control over the raw markdown.

Features:
- WYSIWYG editing powered by Milkdown (ProseMirror + Remark)
- Source mode with CodeMirror 6 for direct markdown editing
- Full CommonMark and GFM support (tables, task lists, footnotes, math)
- Light and dark themes
- Mobile-optimized formatting toolbar
- Local file management — your files stay on your device
- Import files from your device or receive them via share intent
- No account required, no cloud sync, no tracking

Pennivo is MIT-licensed and built entirely in the open. Your writing belongs to you.
